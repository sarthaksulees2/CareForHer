🩸 What is Menstruation?

Menstruation is a natural biological process where the uterus sheds its lining each month.

  • Occurs every 21–35 days.
  • Usually lasts 3–7 days.
  • Begins during puberty.
  • Ends at menopause.

🧼 Menstrual Hygiene

  • Change pads every 4–6 hours.
  • Wash hands regularly.
  • Keep the genital area clean.
  • Dispose products properly.
  • Use clean sanitary products.

🥗 Nutrition During Periods

  • Eat iron-rich foods.
  • Drink plenty of water.
  • Eat fruits and vegetables.
  • Avoid excessive junk food.
  • Consume nuts and seeds.

  • Headaches

🩺 PCOS Awareness

  • Irregular periods
  • Weight gain
  • Acne
  • Hormonal imbalance
  • Consult a doctor if symptoms persist.

🩸 Preventing Anemia

  • Eat leafy vegetables.
  • Consume iron-rich foods.
  • Take vitamin C.
  • Stay hydrated.

❌ Myths vs Facts

  • Myth: Women should not bathe during periods.
    Fact: Bathing improves hygiene.
  • Myth: Exercise should be avoided.
    Fact: Exercise can reduce cramps.

👩‍⚕️ When to See a Doctor

  • Heavy bleeding
  • Severe pain
  • Periods lasting more than 7 days
  • Frequent missed periods
  • Severe dizziness
