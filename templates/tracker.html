🌼 Ovulation Information

Ovulation generally occurs around the 14th day of a 28-day cycle. This is the period when pregnancy is most likely if conception is desired.

💖 Fertile Window

The fertile window usually starts 5 days before ovulation and ends 1 day after ovulation. Tracking this period helps understand reproductive health.

💡 Health Tips
  • Drink plenty of water throughout the day.
  • Maintain a balanced diet rich in iron and vitamins.
  • Track your cycle regularly for accurate predictions.
  • Exercise moderately to reduce cramps and stress.
  • Sleep at least 7–8 hours daily.
  • Consult a healthcare professional for irregular cycles.
