🩺 Emergency Health Tips

  • Stay calm and avoid panic.
  • Contact emergency services immediately.
  • Drink water if feeling weak or dizzy.
  • Rest in a safe and comfortable place.
  • Inform a trusted family member or friend.
  • Seek medical help for severe bleeding or pain.
  • Keep emergency contact numbers saved.

🌸 Women's Health Emergency Guidance

  • Heavy menstrual bleeding requires medical attention.
  • Severe abdominal pain should not be ignored.
  • Persistent dizziness or fainting needs urgent care.
  • Unusual symptoms should be discussed with a doctor.
  • Maintain proper menstrual hygiene during emergencies.

🛡 Safety Recommendations

  • Carry sanitary products when travelling.
  • Keep emergency contacts accessible.
  • Share your location with trusted people when necessary.
  • Visit certified healthcare centers.
  • Do not self-medicate without professional advice.
