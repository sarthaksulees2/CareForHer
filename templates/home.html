About Care For Her

Care For Her aims to improve menstrual health awareness and hygiene management, especially for women living in rural and semi-urban communities. The platform provides easy access to menstrual cycle tracking, educational resources, healthcare guidance, emergency support, and sanitary product accessibility services.
