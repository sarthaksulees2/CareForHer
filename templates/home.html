Why Menstrual Health Matters

Menstrual health awareness helps reduce stigma and improve hygiene practices. Providing proper education empowers women with knowledge to manage their health safely and confidently. MHM Hub aims to provide accessible tools, information, and resources for better menstrual health management, especially in rural areas.
